Description

The Realme C63 is equipped with a 6.74-inch display that offers an HD+ resolution and a 90Hz refresh rate, ensuring smooth visuals and a good viewing experience. The device is powered by the UNISOC T612 chipset, coupled with up to 4GB of RAM and an additional 4GB of dynamic RAM, giving you a balance between efficiency and multitasking. For storage, it offers 128GB of internal memory, expandable via a microSD card.

In the camera department, the Realme C63 features a 50 MP AI main camera, allowing users to capture detailed photos. On the front, there's an 8 MP selfie camera with basic beauty and portrait modes. The device is also IP54 rated, giving it basic protection against dust and water splashes.

The phone is equipped with a large 5000mAh battery and supports 45W fast charging, making sure users stay connected all day long. The software experience is handled by Realme UI Core based on Android, which provides a streamlined user interface.


Specifications

  • Display: 6.74-inch HD+ (1600 x 720 pixels) with a 90Hz refresh rate
  • Processor: UNISOC T612 octa-core chipset
  • RAM: 4GB + 4GB Dynamic RAM
  • Storage: 128GB, expandable via microSD card
  • Rear Camera: 50 MP AI main camera
  • Front Camera: 8 MP selfie camera
  • Battery: 5000mAh with 45W fast charging
  • Operating System: Android-based Realme UI Core
  • Connectivity: Dual 4G standby, Wi-Fi 5, Bluetooth 5.0
  • Dimensions & Weight: 167.26 x 76.67 x 7.74 mm, approximately 189g
  • Colors: Leather Blue, Jade Green
