Frequently Asked Questions

Orders and Shipping

How do I place an order?

+ -

Browse our product catalog, select the items you wish to purchase, add them to your cart, and follow the checkout process. You'll need to provide your shipping details and choose a payment method.

How can I track my order?

+ -

Once your order is shipped, we will send you a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package on our website or the carrier's site.

How long does shipping take?

+ -

Shipping times vary by location. Domestic orders typically arrive within 3-7 business days, while international orders may take 10-15 business days.

Products

Do you offer warranties on gadgets like phones and laptops?

+ -

Yes, all our products come with a manufacturer’s warranty. Details vary by product and brand. Please refer to the product page or contact support for warranty specifics.

What’s included in the box when I buy a phone/laptop?

+ -

The box typically includes the device, charging cables, a power adapter, and a user manual. Any additional accessories will be listed in the product description.

Do you provide technical specifications for your gadgets

+ -

Yes, detailed technical specifications for each product are available on the product page. If you need more information, feel free to reach out to our support team at qibshophelp@gmail.com or at 06-556-9909
