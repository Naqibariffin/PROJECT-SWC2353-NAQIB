Description

The Honor Magic V3 is a flagship foldable smartphone that combines innovation with powerful performance. It features a 7.9-inch main OLED display that folds inwards, providing a tablet-like experience with a stunning 120Hz refresh rate for smooth scrolling and responsiveness. The 6.4-inch external display ensures ease of use when the phone is folded, maintaining the quality with the same high refresh rate.

This flagship device is powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 chipset, paired with 12GB or 16GB of LPDDR5X RAM, ensuring seamless performance and efficient multitasking. For storage, it offers up to 1TB of UFS 4.0 storage, providing ample space for apps, media, and files.

The Magic V3's camera setup includes a 50 MP main camera with optical image stabilization, a 50 MP telephoto lens with 3.5x optical zoom, and a 40 MP ultra-wide lens, delivering versatile photography options. The 20 MP front camera is great for selfies and 4K video calls. The phone supports 4K video recording with both front and rear cameras.

    • Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 3

    • Storage: 256GB / 512GB / 1TB UFS 4.0 (non-expandable)
    • Rear Camera: 50 MP main (OIS), 50 MP telephoto (3.5x zoom), 40 MP ultra-wide
