Description

The Samsung Galaxy A06 is a value-packed smartphone that offers essential features for those seeking reliability on a budget. With its 6.7-inch PLS LCD display, the phone delivers clear and vibrant visuals at an HD+ resolution. It has a dual-camera system, including a 50 MP main sensor for high-quality photos and a 2 MP depth sensor to add bokeh effects to your portraits. The 8 MP front-facing camera is ideal for selfies and video calls.

Powered by the MediaTek Helio G85 processor, the Galaxy A06 handles everyday tasks smoothly, whether you're browsing, streaming, or using apps. The device comes with 4GB or 6GB of RAM, along with 64GB or 128GB of internal storage, which can be expanded up to 1TB via a dedicated microSD slot. It runs on Android 14 with Samsung’s One UI Core for a clean and efficient software experience. With a 5000mAh battery and 25W fast charging, the Galaxy A06 ensures all-day usage and quick recharging.


Specifications

  • Display: 6.7-inch PLS LCD, 720 x 1600 pixels
  • Processor: MediaTek Helio G85
  • RAM: 4GB / 6GB
  • Storage: 64GB / 128GB (expandable up to 1TB via microSD)
  • Rear Camera: 50 MP main, 2 MP depth sensor
  • Front Camera: 8 MP
  • Battery: 5000mAh with 25W fast charging
  • Operating System: Android 14 with One UI Core
  • Connectivity: 4G, Wi-Fi 5, Bluetooth 5.3, USB Type-C
  • Dimensions: 167.3 x 77.3 x 8 mm, 189 g
  • Colors: Black,Mint Green
