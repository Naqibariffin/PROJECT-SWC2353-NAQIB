Description

The Samsung Galaxy S24 FE is designed for users seeking a blend of performance and affordability without compromising on essential features. It boasts a vibrant 6.7-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display with a resolution of 1080 x 2340 pixels and a smooth 120Hz refresh rate, ensuring that every interaction is fluid and responsive. With a peak brightness of 1900 nits, it provides excellent visibility even in bright sunlight.

Powered by the Exynos 2400e chipset (or Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 in some regions), paired with 8GB of RAM, the Galaxy S24 FE handles multitasking and demanding applications with ease. Its versatile camera system includes a 50 MP main camera, a 12 MP ultra-wide lens, and an 8 MP telephoto lens with 3x optical zoom, enhanced by AI capabilities for improved photo quality and editing options.

The device is equipped with a robust 4,700 mAh battery, supporting 25W wired charging, fast wireless charging, and Wireless PowerShare. Running on Android 14 with One UI 6.1, it delivers a seamless user experience, while its IP68 rating ensures protection against dust and water.


Specifications

    • Display: 6.7-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X, 1080 x 2340 pixels, 120Hz refresh rate, peak brightness 1900 nits
    • Processor: Exynos 2400e (Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 in select regions)
    • RAM: 8GB
    • Storage: 128GB / 256GB (non-expandable)
    • Rear Camera: 50 MP main, 12 MP ultra-wide, 8 MP telephoto (3x optical zoom)
    • Front Camera: 10 MP
    • Battery: 4,700 mAh with 25W fast charging, wireless charging, and Wireless PowerShare
    • Operating System: Android 14 with One UI 6.1
    • Connectivity: 5G, Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.3, USB Type-C
    • Dimensions: 162 x 77.3 x 8 mm, 213 g
    • Colors: Blue, Graphite, Gray
