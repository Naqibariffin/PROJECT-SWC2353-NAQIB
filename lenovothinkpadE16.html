Description

The Lenovo ThinkPad E16 is a versatile business laptop designed for productivity and performance. It features a 16-inch IPS display with a resolution of 1920 x 1200 pixels, providing clear visuals and vibrant colors. The laptop is powered by the latest Intel or AMD processors, ensuring smooth multitasking and efficient performance. With up to 40GB of RAM and various SSD storage options, it meets the needs of demanding users. Built with durability in mind, the ThinkPad E16 is equipped with a robust chassis and a comfortable keyboard, making it suitable for extended work sessions.


Specifications

  • Display: 16-inch IPS, 1920 x 1200 pixels
  • Processor: Intel Core i3/i5/i7 or AMD Ryzen 3/5/7
  • RAM: Up to 40GB DDR4
  • Storage: Up to 2TB SSD (PCIe NVMe)
  • Graphics: Integrated Intel UHD or NVIDIA GeForce MX550
  • Battery: 47Wh / 57Wh, fast charging
  • Operating System: Windows 11 Pro
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.2, USB-C, HDMI
  • Weight: Approximately 1.76 kg (3.88 lbs)
  • Dimensions: 356.1 x 247.5 x 19.85 mm
