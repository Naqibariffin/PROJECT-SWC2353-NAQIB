Description

The iPhone 15 Pro takes innovation to new heights, combining cutting-edge technology with a sleek design. Its 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display brings vibrant colors and sharp details to everything you do. Powered by the A16 Bionic chip, the iPhone 15 Pro delivers lightning-fast performance, whether you're multitasking, gaming, or capturing photos.

With a powerful triple-camera system, including a 48 MP main camera, the iPhone 15 Pro is designed for photography enthusiasts. It also supports 5G connectivity for ultra-fast browsing and streaming, along with improved battery life to keep you connected all day. The aerospace-grade aluminum frame and Ceramic Shield front cover offer exceptional durability, while iOS 17 ensures a smooth, intuitive experience.


Specifications

  • Display: 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR with ProMotion
  • Processor: A16 Bionic chip with 6-core CPU and 5-core GPU
  • Camera System: 48 MP Main, 12 MP Ultra-wide, 12 MP Telephoto
  • Battery Life: Up to 23 hours of video playback
  • Storage Options: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, 1TB
  • Connectivity: 5G, Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.3
  • Materials: Aerospace-grade aluminum frame, Ceramic Shield front cover
  • Water Resistance: IP68 (up to 6 meters for 30 minutes)
  • Operating System: iOS 17
