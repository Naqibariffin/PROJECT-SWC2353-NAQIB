Description

The Samsung Galaxy Z Flip 5 redefines what a smartphone can be with its innovative foldable design and powerful features. Featuring a 6.7-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display, the Z Flip 5 provides stunning visuals and vibrant colors for an immersive experience. The cover display has been upgraded to a 3.4-inch Super AMOLED display, allowing for quick access to notifications, music controls, and widgets without unfolding the device.

Powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 processor, the Z Flip 5 delivers exceptional performance, whether you’re multitasking, gaming, or streaming content. The dual-camera system includes a 12 MP wide and a 12 MP ultra-wide lens, enabling you to capture stunning photos and videos. With its compact and stylish design, advanced features, and enhanced durability, the Galaxy Z Flip 5 is a perfect choice for those seeking a modern, flexible smartphone experience.


Specifications

  • Display: 6.7-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X (main), 3.4-inch Super AMOLED (cover)
  • Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 2
  • Camera System: Dual 12 MP (wide) + 12 MP (ultra-wide)
  • Battery Capacity: 3,700 mAh
  • Storage Options: 256GB, 512GB
  • RAM: 8GB
  • Connectivity: 5G, Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.3
  • Materials: Armor Aluminum frame, Gorilla Glass Victus 2 front and back
  • Water Resistance: IPX8
  • Operating System: Android 13 with One UI 5.1
  • Colors: Black, Cream, Lavender, Mint
