Description

The HP Omen 16 is a powerful gaming laptop designed for immersive performance and cutting-edge graphics. Featuring a 16.1-inch QHD display with a 165 Hz refresh rate and a 3 ms response time, it delivers smooth visuals that enhance the gaming experience. Whether you're engaged in fast-paced action or graphically demanding tasks, the Omen 16 excels with its robust hardware options, including Intel's 14th generation processors or AMD Ryzen 9 processors, paired with NVIDIA GeForce RTX graphics.

This laptop is equipped with advanced cooling technology to maintain performance during extended gaming sessions. The Omen Gaming Hub allows for performance optimization and customization to suit individual gaming styles. With high-speed RAM and ample SSD storage, the Omen 16 is built for multitasking and quick load times, ensuring that gamers stay ahead of the competition.


Specifications

  • Display: 16.1-inch QHD (2560 x 1600), 165 Hz, 3 ms response time, IPS
  • Processor: Up to Intel® Core™ i7-13700H
  • Graphics: Up to NVIDIA® GeForce RTX™ 3070 Ti (8 GB GDDR6) or AMD Radeon™ RX 6600M
  • RAM: Up to 32 GB DDR5-4800 MHz
  • Storage: Up to 1 TB PCIe® Gen4 NVMe™ SSD
  • Operating System: Up to Windows 11 Home
  • Battery: 4-cell, 70 Wh Li-ion with HP Fast Charge technology
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.2, multiple USB ports, HDMI 2.1
  • Audio: Bang & Olufsen dual speakers
  • Weight: Approximately 2.2 kg (4.85 lbs)
  • Colors: Shadow Black
