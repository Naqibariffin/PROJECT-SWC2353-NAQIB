Description

The iPhone 16 is designed for those who seek cutting-edge technology combined with sleek design. Featuring a stunning 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR display, every detail comes to life with remarkable clarity. Powered by the advanced A17 Bionic chip, the iPhone 16 delivers top-tier performance, whether you're multitasking, gaming, or capturing photos with its enhanced camera system.

Built to last, the iPhone 16 is crafted with a robust Titanium frame and a durable Ceramic Shield front cover, offering both beauty and strength. With 5G connectivity, extended battery life, and water resistance, the iPhone 16 offers a seamless experience. Experience iOS 18 like never before with the iPhone 16 – where innovation meets elegance.


Specifications

  • Display: 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR with ProMotion
  • Processor: A17 Bionic chip with 6-core CPU and 6-core GPU
  • Camera System: 48 MP Main, 12 MP Ultra-wide, 12 MP Telephoto
  • Battery Life: Up to 28 hours of talk time
  • Storage Options: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, 1TB

  • Materials: Titanium frame, Ceramic Shield front cover
  • Water Resistance: IP68 (up to 6 meters for 30 minutes)
  • Operating System: iOS 18
