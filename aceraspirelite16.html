Description

The Acer Aspire Lite 16 is a sleek and budget-friendly laptop designed for productivity and everyday use. It features a 16-inch WUXGA IPS display with a resolution of 1920x1200, delivering clear visuals with an anti-glare finish. The laptop is powered by the 13th Gen Intel Core i5-13500H processor, making it capable of handling multitasking, light gaming, and professional work efficiently. It includes 16GB of DDR4 RAM and a 1TB NVMe SSD for quick data access and ample storage.

For connectivity, it offers WiFi 6E, Bluetooth 5.1, and several ports including Thunderbolt 4 and USB 3.2 Gen 1 Type-A. The 62.7Wh battery provides decent battery life, and it is paired with a 100W adapter for fast charging. The laptop also comes with an HD webcam, stereo speakers, and dual-array microphones for a complete multimedia experience


Specifications

  • Display: 16-inch WUXGA (1920 x 1200 pixels) IPS LCD, 60Hz refresh rate, anti-glare
  • Processor: 13th Gen Intel Core i5-13500H
  • RAM: 16GB DDR4
  • Storage: 1TB M.2 NVMe SSD
  • Graphics: Intel Integrated UHD Graphics
  • Battery: 62.7Wh, supports 100W fast charging
  • Operating System: Windows 11 Home
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.1, Thunderbolt 4, USB-A, USB-C, HDMI, Ethernet
  • Dimensions & Weight: 360.4 x 239.3 x 19.9 mm, 1.81 kg
  • Other Features: HD webcam, dual speakers, dual-array microphones, TPM 2.0, chiclet keyboard
