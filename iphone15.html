Description

The iPhone 15 features a stunning design with a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display, offering vibrant colors and incredible detail for a superior viewing experience. Powered by the A16 Bionic chip, the iPhone 15 ensures smooth performance whether you're gaming, streaming, or using demanding apps.

Equipped with a dual-camera system, including a 48 MP main camera, the iPhone 15 captures stunning photos in any lighting condition. It also supports advanced features like Photonic Engine for improved low-light performance and 5G connectivity for fast internet access. The sleek design, enhanced battery life, and the intuitive iOS 17 make the iPhone 15 a top choice for anyone looking to stay connected and enjoy premium features.


Specifications

  • Display: 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR
  • Processor: A16 Bionic chip with 6-core CPU and 5-core GPU
  • Camera System: 48 MP Main, 12 MP Ultra-wide
  • Battery Life: Up to 20 hours of video playback
  • Storage Options: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB
  • Connectivity: 5G, Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.3
  • Materials: Aluminum frame, Ceramic Shield front cover
  • Water Resistance: IP68 (up to 6 meters for 30 minutes)
  • Operating System: iOS 17
  • Colors: Black, Blue, Pink, Yellow, Green
