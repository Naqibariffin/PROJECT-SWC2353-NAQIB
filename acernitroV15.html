Description

The Acer Nitro V 15 is designed for gamers seeking a powerful and budget-friendly laptop. Featuring a 15.6-inch Full HD IPS display, it offers vibrant visuals with a refresh rate of up to 165 Hz, ensuring smooth gameplay. The laptop is powered by the latest 13th Gen Intel® Core™ processors and NVIDIA® GeForce RTX™ 40 Series GPUs, providing exceptional performance for gaming, content creation, and multitasking.

With up to 32 GB of DDR5 RAM and a maximum of 2 TB PCIe M.2 SSD storage, the Nitro V 15 handles demanding applications with ease. Its dual fan cooling system keeps the internals cool during intense gaming sessions. The laptop also includes DTS Ultra audio for immersive sound and comes pre-installed with Windows 11, enhancing the overall user experience.


Specifications

  • Display: 15.6-inch Full HD IPS, 1920 x 1080 pixels, up to 165 Hz refresh rate
  • Processor: Up to 13th Gen Intel® Core™ i7
  • Graphics: Up to NVIDIA® GeForce RTX™ 4050
  • RAM: Up to 32 GB DDR5
  • Storage: Up to 2 TB PCIe M.2 SSD
  • Battery: Up to 8 hours of usage (varies by configuration)
  • Operating System: Windows 11
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6, Thunderbolt™ 4, USB Type-C, HDMI
  • Dimensions: 363.4 x 248 x 23.5 mm
  • Weight: Approximately 2.5 kg
  • Colors: Black with Miami Vice accent
